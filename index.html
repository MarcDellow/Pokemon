Welcome to Pokemon 151


Step back in time and relive the magic of the very first Pokémon card set! This site is dedicated to showcasing high-quality images of the original Pokémon Trading Card Game (TCG) set, released in 1996 (Japan) and 1999 (International). Whether you're a nostalgic collector, a TCG enthusiast, or just discovering the roots of Pokémon cards, explore the iconic artwork and legendary cards that started it all. Gotta catch 'em all? Start your journey now!
